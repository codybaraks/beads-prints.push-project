When moving to a new home, office or country, the hassle, time expense and costs associated with purchasing furniture can be high. Whether you utilize budget furniture sellers or not, furnishing an entire home or office is going to take time: time to pick furniture, buy it, have it delivered, arrange it, and in some cases, assemble it. This is not to mention to cost of cleaning and upkeep in the (inevitable) event that someone spills, rips or damages your furniture. Renting your furniture allows you to live in a beautifully furnished space, without the same cost and hassle of ownership.
Unless we explicitly note this in our rental proposal, all furniture items are rented to you in brand-new condition. Can you help me choose furniture? Our team of professional furniture rental consultants is here for you, equipped with specialized training in interior décor and design. We offer intelligent expertise on all our products and packages. We are happy to use our experience to help you create the home that fits your needs, taste and budget.
There are no “hidden” or additional costs. We pride ourselves on financial and document transparency. Our monthly rental fees include the delivery and assembly of furniture and appliances. When you move out, we disassemble your furniture as part of your rental fee.
We understand that there are extenuating circumstances in life, and we may be able to offer some flexibility if the need arises. If you have trouble with your lease for any reason, please give us a call
You have the option to purchase your Furniture Leasing Corporation products at a discounted rate at the end of your lease. Monthly rental payments do not count towards end of lease purchases.
